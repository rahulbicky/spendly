Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your financial records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside of operating the app on your behalf.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely on our servers and is associated only with your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers. Google Fonts are loaded from Google's servers to render the app's typography — this is the only external service the app contacts, and it does not receive any of your account or expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries promptly.
